About

Maria Di Bello is a Product Designer currently at comiXology in NYC.

Conceptualizing and leading impactful design, she focuses on creativity, customer engagement and digital strategy to create innovative experiences. A background in both B2B and B2C business approaches, as well as the capabilities to take a product and own it from end to end in an agile environment.
Experience collaborating with engineers/developers both in person and offshore, project stakeholders, managers, agencies, as well as copywriters, creative directors and fellow designers.Experience with native applications for iOS and Android.
